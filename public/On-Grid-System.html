REDUCE YOUR ELECTRICITY BILLS WITH PHOTON ELECTRONICS ON-GRID SYSTEM PACKAGES


Photon Electronics offers a wide range of on-grid system packages to meet your electricity needs. Our on-grid system packages are designed to reduce your electricity bills and increase your energy savings.


To provide our customers with convenience, Photon Electronics offers a range of different on-grid solar system packages at affordable prices. The Photon On-Grid Solar system price in Pakistan, including net metering, starts at just Rs. 995,000 for a 5kW on-grid system. As the system size increases, it becomes even more cost-effective; our 10kW on-grid system is available for just Rs. 1,800,000, and the price for a 25kW on-grid system is only Rs. 2,990,000.
